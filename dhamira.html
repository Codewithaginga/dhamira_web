About us

DHAMIRA MOJA CBO is a youth led organisation committed to creating a society where all individuals, regardless of gender, can live free from suffering and injustice. We strive for gender equality, challenging oppressive systems, and empowering marginalised communities through sustainable development initiatives. As an organisation, we believe that together, we can create lasting change and build a more equitable world. The organisation is keen to implement programs that prioritise gender equality, women's empowerment, and social justice. Our initiatives focus on education, health, economic empowerment, environmental sustainability and advocating for good governance and social social accountability.

Our primary focus is on women, especially those facing multiple vulnerabilities, as well as orphans, youth, and other marginalised individuals. By addressing the specific needs and challenges faced by each target group, we aim to uplift the entire community recognizing that achieving gender equality is crucial for sustainable development. As a feminist organisation, we believe in challenging patriarchal norms and dismantling systems of oppression that perpetuate gender inequality. By empowering girls, women, the youth and marginalised individuals, we strive to create a more just and inclusive society where everyone can thrive and sustainable development can take place

VISION

A society free of mankind's suffering and injustices.

MISSION:

To provide structural and sustainable support and services to alleviate poverty and improve the standard of living for all community members, especially women, orphans, youth, and marginalised individuals.

OBJECTIVE

To promote sustainable livelihoods and empower girls, women, the youth, orphans and marginalised individuals in Busia County to overcome gender based barriers and discrimination so that they can live healthy, productive and fulfilling lives.

Given our mandate in the community, we achieve our goals and objective through the following:

Education & Mentorship
Health & Gender Equality
Climate Change & Disaster Management
Adovacy & Good Governance
  • Education And : We provide scholarships and education programs that promote feminist values, challenging traditional gender roles and empowering girls and women to become agents of change.
  • Gender-Sensitive Empowerment: We offer capacity-building programs, access to resources, and mentorship opportunities, addressing the unique challenges faced by women, girls, the youth and marginalised individuals.
  • Health and Reproductive Rights: We promote comprehensive reproductive health education, including menstrual hygiene management, and advocate for the rights and well-being of women and girls.
  • Intersectional Approach: We recognize and address the multiple layers of oppression faced by marginalised individuals, ensuring that our programs are inclusive and responsive to their specific needs.
  • Advocacy and Policy Change: We engage in advocacy efforts to influence policies and laws that promote gender equality, challenging discriminatory practices and promoting women's rights.
  • Community Engagement: We facilitate community dialogues, interfaith dialogues, and radio talk shows, creating spaces for open discussions on gender equality, feminism, and the prevention of gender-based violence.
  • Environmental Sustainability: We integrate feminist perspectives in our climate change and disaster management initiatives, recognizing the disproportionate impact on women and advocating for gender-responsive strategies.
