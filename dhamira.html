About us

DHAMIRA MOJA CBO is a proud feminist organisation committed to creating a society where all individuals, regardless of gender, can live free from suffering and injustice. We strive for gender equality, challenging oppressive systems, and empowering marginalised communities through sustainable development initiatives. As an organisation, we believe that together, we can create lasting change and build a more equitable world. The organisation is keen to implement programs that prioritise gender equality, women's empowerment, and social justice. Our initiatives focus on education, health, economic empowerment, environmental sustainability and advocating for good governance and social social accountability, all underpinned by feminist principles.
